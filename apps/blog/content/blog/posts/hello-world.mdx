This is the first post on my personal blog, scaffolded inside the **EverythingMe** monorepo.

## What lives here

- **Blog** — Fumadocs MDX content in `content/blog/`
- **Re-event the wheel** — rebuilding existing apps with my own approach
- **Studying** — small apps to learn new technology hands-on
- **Foundation libs** — shared auth, database, UI, and config

## Lessons folder rule

Every revent-the-wheel and studying project keeps a `lessons/` folder next to its source code to document findings and decisions.

```text
apps/revent-the-wheel/todo-clone/
├── lessons/
├── src/
└── package.json
```

Happy building.
